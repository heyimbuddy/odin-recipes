Waffles

Description

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Vanilla
  • Cooking Spray

Steps

  1. Make the better by whisking the eggs, then adding the flour, milk, and oil.
  2. Whisk in the sugar. Stir in the remaining ingredients.
  3. Pour the batter onto the hot, prepared waffle iron.
  4. Cook until golden brown and repeat with the remaining batter.
